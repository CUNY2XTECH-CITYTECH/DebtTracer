About Us

We believe that personal finance should be simple, smart, and empowering.

Our mission is to build a seamless financial tracking experience that helps people understand their money — and take control of it. Whether it’s managing everyday expenses, tracking income, or tackling debt, our platform is designed to support financial clarity at every level.

This application was developed by a passionate team of innovators — Anna, Borys, Tonny, Sutibro, and Christopher G — with a shared vision to transform budgeting into a more intuitive and data-driven process.

Built using modern web technologies, our goal is to not just track numbers, but to tell the story behind them. We’re continuously evolving the platform to offer smarter insights, user-friendly features, and powerful tools to help users shape a better financial future.
